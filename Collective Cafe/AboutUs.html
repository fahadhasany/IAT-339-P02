About Us

Our Story

Three minds with the same eccentric idea, The Collective Café was created. The Collective Café wanted to create more than just a place to grab a cup of coffee but a space for a community of people to enjoy coffee that they learned to brew themselves.

The Collective Cafe started off in a small location in Surrey B.C and is now opening it's 2nd location in Downtown Vancouver!
